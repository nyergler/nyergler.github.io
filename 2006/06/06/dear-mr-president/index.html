Dear Mr. President,

6 June 2006

I understand that you are quite concerned about judicial activism . I appreciate your concern regarding historical precedent and original intent . Perhaps you could [STRIKEOUT:re-]read the Federalist Papers for additional perspective. In particular I recommend Number 10 . I’m sure you’ll find Madison’s thoughts on the tyranny of the majority fascinating. One reading is that your majority [STRIKEOUT:and political capital] (sorry, I forgot that where once there was a surplus there is now a deficit) justify the Federal Marriage Amendment. I submit that an alternate reading is that judges are simply protecting a minority of the population from said tyranny. Since you are “the decider “, I eagerly await your decision.

Patriotically,

Nathan
